Mathematical Formulas

1. Scalar Derivative with respect to a Vector
$$
\frac{\partial (\mathbf{a}^T \mathbf{x})}{\partial \mathbf{x}} = \mathbf{a}
$$
2. Quadratic Form Derivative with respect to a Vector
$$
\frac{\partial (\mathbf{x}^T \mathbf{A} \mathbf{x})}{\partial \mathbf{x}} = (\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{A}^T) \mathbf{x}
$$
3. Linear Form Derivative with respect to a Vector
$$
\frac{\partial (\mathbf{b}^T \mathbf{x})}{\partial \mathbf{x}} = \mathbf{b}
$$
4. Trace Derivative with respect to a Matrix
$$
\frac{\partial \mathrm{tr}(\mathbf{A}\mathbf{X})}{\partial \mathbf{X}} = \mathbf{A}^T
$$
5. Quadratic Form Derivative with respect to a Matrix
$$
\frac{\partial (\mathbf{x}^T \mathbf{A} \mathbf{x})}{\partial \mathbf{A}} = \mathbf{x} \mathbf{x}^T
$$
6. Frobenius Norm Derivative
$$
\frac{\partial |\mathbf{X}|_F^2}{\partial \mathbf{X}} = 2\mathbf{X}
$$
7. Diagonal Matrix Derivative with respect to a Vector
$$
\frac{\partial (\mathbf{x}^T \mathrm{diag}(\mathbf{a}) \mathbf{x})}{\partial \mathbf{x}} = 2 \mathrm{diag}(\mathbf{a}) \mathbf{x}
$$
8. Matrix Product Derivative with respect to a Matrix
$$
\frac{\partial (\mathbf{X}\mathbf{A})}{\partial \mathbf{X}} = \mathbf{A}^T
$$
9. Cross-Entropy Loss Derivative with respect to the Input
$$
\frac{\partial \text{Cross Entropy}(y, \hat{y})}{\partial \hat{y}} = -\frac{y}{\hat{y}} + \frac{1 - y}{1 - \hat{y}}
$$
10. Softmax Derivative with respect to the Input
$$
\frac{\partial \sigma(\mathbf{z})_i}{\partial z_j} = \sigma(\mathbf{z})i (\delta{ij} - \sigma(\mathbf{z})_j)
$$
