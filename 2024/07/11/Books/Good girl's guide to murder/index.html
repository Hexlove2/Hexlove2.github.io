My thoughts:

First and foremost, I read this book because of this tv series, and most importantly, I am a fan of Emma Myers, her performance in this series is excellent.

I have finished this book so I want to have a conclusion. TBH, I think this book is good but not perfect. Specifically, some characters’ actions seem illogical. The biggest problem is that those the villains in reality wouldn’t reveal the truth so easily. For instance, Naomi, she told pip that they had hit someone years ago, ok, it makes sense, but then Max revealed more truth easily and even admitted that he had raped someone, that’s insane. And, Mr.Ward, what’s wrong with him, he just needs to say I have no clue, I haven’t kill anyone, this girl is so pathtic that I settled her here and offered her food, that’s it. They don’t have proof, that’s the problem. But what he did is tell everything to Pip, yes, I killed Sal, I am the murder. That’s insane and ridiculous. Finally, Becca Bell, what’s wrong with her? She did the same thing, she told Pip everything even though there was no evidence, she knew it, and she wanted to kill Pip instead of just cheating. What the fuck.

Emmm, but it also has a lot of merits, if I don’t consider it as a detective fiction, I gained an understanding of student’s life and culture there, so I still like it.

Main Characters and Their Relationships:

  1. Pippa Fitz-Amobi (Pip):

​ • Sal Singh: Investigates his alleged involvement in Andie Bell’s murder.

​ • Andie Bell: The murder victim whose case Pip is investigating.

​ • Ravi Singh: Sal’s brother, who assists Pip in her investigation.

​ • Cara Ward: Pip’s best friend.

​ • Naomi Ward: Cara’s older sister and Pip’s older friend.

​ • Stanley Forbes: A local reporter who covers the case.

  1. Andie Bell:

​ • Sal Singh: Her boyfriend, accused of her murder.

​ • Jason Bell: Andie’s father.

​ • Becca Bell: Andie’s younger sister.

​ • Elliot Ward: A teacher at Pip’s school who is connected to the case.

  1. Sal Singh:

​ • Ravi Singh: His brother, who believes in Sal’s innocence.

​ • Pippa Fitz-Amobi: The protagonist who re-investigates the case.

  1. Ravi Singh:

​ • Pippa Fitz-Amobi: Collaborates with her to uncover the truth about Sal and Andie’s case.

  1. Cara Ward:

​ • Pippa Fitz-Amobi: Best friends.

​ • Naomi Ward: Her older sister, connected to the case.

  1. Naomi Ward:

​ • Cara Ward: Her younger sister.

​ • Pippa Fitz-Amobi: Connected through their shared interest in the case.

  1. Stanley Forbes:

​ • Pippa Fitz-Amobi: Covers the developments in her investigation.

  1. Jason Bell:

​ • Andie Bell: His daughter.

​ • Becca Bell: His younger daughter.

  1. Becca Bell:

​ • Andie Bell: Her older sister.

​ • Pippa Fitz-Amobi: Involved in the investigation through her family.

  1. Elliot Ward:

​ • Naomi Ward: His daughter.

​ • Pippa Fitz-Amobi: Connected through the investigation and his teaching role.
